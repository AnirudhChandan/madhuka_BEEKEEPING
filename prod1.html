RAW HONEY

madhūka NATURELLE is a premium Raw Honey responsibly extracted from our own beehives placed placed in fields around the Shivalik & Aravali ranges and tended by experienced beekeepers using responsible practices to bring out a safe food product. It is unheated, unfiltered, unpasteurised and without any preservatives or artificial flavours/colours to avoid stripping off beneficial qualities of honey.

RAW & UNPASTEURISED

To maintain the RAW status of honey, we don't expose honey to temperatures above 35° centigrade and maintain the honey as it exists in the beehive. To keep it raw we don't apply fine filtration processes, as this removes pollen, enzymes, nutritious bee bread and minerals
